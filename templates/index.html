Our Approach

How VQ-VAE-2 Enables Coverless Steganography

Our research utilizes the discrete codebook representation of VQ-VAE-2 to achieve direct audio generation with embedded secret messages. Unlike conventional steganography that alters existing files, our approach generates entirely new audio that inherently contains the hidden information.

By mapping secret data to the latent space of the model, we create audio files that are both high-quality and secure, with no detectable artifacts from data embedding.

The project encompasses model training with extensive audio datasets, secret data mapping strategies, and robust evaluation metrics for both audio quality and information retrieval accuracy.
