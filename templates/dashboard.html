How It Works

Audio steganography is the science of hiding secret data within audio files. Unlike encryption, which makes data unreadable but noticeable, steganography conceals the very existence of the secret message.

Our system uses advanced techniques to encode your message in high-frequency components of a pleasant musical melody, making it virtually undetectable to casual listeners.

Our system uses multiple parallel frequency pairs in the 17-20kHz range to encode data bits. This approach provides:

  • High data capacity (multiple bits encoded simultaneously)
  • Resilience to audio processing
  • Natural-sounding carrier audio
  • Psychoacoustic masking for optimal hiding

The musical component is generated using algorithmic composition with chord progressions, melodies, and harmonics to create pleasant background music.

While our audio steganography provides excellent concealment, please note:

  • The message is hidden, but not encrypted. For maximum security, encrypt your message before hiding it.
  • Audio compression (MP3, AAC) may damage the hidden data. Always use uncompressed WAV files.
